AMANDA BARBATO photography

photography, to enjoy the present.

My very first camera was given to me on my seventh Birthday, where you only get one flash bulb and hope for the best. I became fasinated about photography, the experiences, and the magic it holds.

My uncle, upgraded me with his old camera, lenes, and a flash, as a present, since he owned his own camera store. It was then, I started to photograph my environment around me, which was local punk shows, my surrounding neighborhoods, and my friends.

In college, I spent my weekends in the darkroom, exploring with different chemicals, manipulations, film devlopment and printing. It was a magical experience, to be in control, or to fail when film was not developed correctly, and all my memories of the present time, were earsed.
