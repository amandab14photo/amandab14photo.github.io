ABOUT ME_My very first camera was given to me on my seventh birthday and was fasinated by the magic it holds. My uncle, who owned his own photo store, upgraded me from point and shoot, to his old, manual, heavy camera, with fixed lenes, and a flash, as a present. It was in junior year of high school, where I started to photograph the environment around me, which was local punk shows, my surrounding neighborhoods, and my friends. In college, I spent my weekends in the darkroom, exploring with different chemicals, manipulations, film devlopment, and printing. It was a magical experience, to be in control, and learned to fail when film was not developed correctly, and all my memories of the present time, were earsed.
